Cleaning up your twitter mess

As we spend more time online, quality of that time matters a lot. What we hear, read and listen on the social networks influence our opinions.

With assumption that none of us wants to surround herself with insults and aggressive opinions, this tool was created to help you analyze tweets of your friends and tries to measure toxicity of their language. That means that it takes tweets of users you follow and Perspective API determines toxicity of their content.

Note that the data model is far form perfect and this project is just experiment. Created in 2019 by Milan Å vehla
